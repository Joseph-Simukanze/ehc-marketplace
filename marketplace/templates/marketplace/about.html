Our Story

Born from a student project at Evelynhone College, EHC Marketplace has grown into the premier platform connecting students across campus. What started as a simple idea to help classmates buy and sell textbooks has blossomed into a vibrant community marketplace.

Our Mission

To create a secure, student-friendly marketplace that makes campus life more affordable and convenient.
